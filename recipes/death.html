Braised DeathClaw Steak

This ones only for the true adventurer. Possibly the most dangerous to aquire ingredient will do. Meat taken fresh from a recently slain DeathClaw.

Once eaten this steak will make you feel as agile as an olympian.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 pounds deathclaw belly
  • 1 TBS granulated sugar
  • 2 TBS light brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 cup sake
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 2 TBS rice vinegar
  • 3 TBS Hoisin sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • one 3- inch piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 lemongrass stalks3 bay leaves
  • 3 star anise
  • 3 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 dried chiles de arbol (or other pepper such as cayenne)
  • 1 TBS peppercorns

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325F. Place the deathclaw belly in a larg pot and cover with cold water over medium-high heat bring to a boil then reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 15minutes. REmove any foam that floats to the top. Remove the belly from the water and carefully cut into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Add the granulated sugar, light brown-sugar and 1/2 cup water to a large, ovenproof pot and place over medium high heat. Whisk together until the sugar has dissolved then add the pork belly to the pot and stir to coat. Add all the remaining ingredients to the pot and bring to a low boil.
  3. Cover the pot with an ovenproof lid and transfer to the oven bake for 1 hour. Increase the heat of the oven to 375F and remove the cover. Bake for 1 hour more stirring the contents every 15minutes.
  4. Remove the pot from the oven and liet it rest on the stove. Take a cup of the sauce from the pot and pour it into a small sauce pan over high heat. Cook until the sauce reduces and thickens. Toss the belly pieces into the reduced sauce and serve.
