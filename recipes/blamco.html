Blamco Mac & Cheese

The staple of the famous industrial powerhouse BLAMCO. A delicate and delicious dish. You will feel your health restored as the spice from the radiation kicks in.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups water
  • 12 ounces evaporated milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 1/2 cups cheddar cheese,grated
  • 1 1/2 cups monterey jack cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons butter, cubed
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Instuctions

  1. Add 3 cups of the water, the evaporated milk, and macaroni together in a large skillet. Heat over medium high heat, stirring constantly to prevent scorching, until the macaroni is tender and the liquid has thickened slightly.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk the cornstarch with the dry mustard and remaining 1/2 cup of water. Add to the skillet and continue to cook for a few minutes.
  3. Remove the skillet from the heat and slowly stir in the cheese and butter. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
